ğŸ“š Essential Vocabulary

Respect

Definition: Treating others with consideration, listening to their "no", valuing their opinions.

Example: "It's okay if you don't want to go out tonight!"

Boundaries

Definition: Your personal limits - what makes you feel good and what doesn't. You have the right to have them!

Example: "I don't like it when you look at my phone."

Manipulation

Definition: When someone makes you feel guilty or wrong to get what they want.

Example: "If you really loved me, you would..."

Control

Definition: When someone decides for you, isolates you, or controls what you do and who you see.

Example: "You can't go out with your friends."

Gaslighting

Definition: Making someone doubt their own perception of reality.

Example: "That never happened, you're making it up!"

Healthy Relationship

Definition: Based on mutual respect, trust, honest communication and freedom.

Example: Both people support each other and grow together.
